Love Spell

This spell is especially powerful when focused on cultivating self-love, healing, and personal harmony.

Materials

  • A pink candle (or white if pink is unavailable)
  • Rose petals (fresh or dried)
  • Honey or sugar (to sweeten your intentions)
  • A small mirror (to reflect loving energy back to yourself)
  • Optional: a small item that makes you feel confident (jewelry, charm, photo)

Steps

  1. Prepare your space and light the pink candle with care and intention.
  2. Place the mirror in front of you and scatter the rose petals around it.
  3. Put a small dab of honey or sprinkle of sugar on the mirror or in a small dish nearby.
  4. Gaze into the mirror, softly affirming words of love and acceptance to yourself.
  5. Hold your confidence item while the candle burns, letting the warmth fill you with appreciation and self-worth.
