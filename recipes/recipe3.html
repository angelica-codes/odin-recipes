Abundance Spell

This spell is intended to invite prosperity, attract opportunities, and align your energy with the flow of abundance in all forms.

Materials

  • A green candle (for prosperity)
  • A coin or bill (symbol of physical wealth)
  • Cinnamon or basil (for attraction)
  • A small dish or bowl (to contain the spell)
  • Optional: citrine or pyrite crystal (to amplify abundance energy)

Steps

  1. Place your bowl or dish in the center of your workspace and light the green candle.
  2. Sprinkle the cinnamon or basil into the bowl, focusing on your intention to attract abundance.
  3. Add the coin or bill into the bowl, symbolizing your readiness to receive wealth in all forms.
  4. Speak an affirmation or short chant inviting abundance into your life while gazing at the flame.
  5. Let the candle burn down safely, then place the contents somewhere special until your goal is met.
