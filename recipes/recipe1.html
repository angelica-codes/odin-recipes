Protection Spell

This simple ritual is designed to center your energy, banish negativity, and create a gentle barrier of spiritual protection around you.

Materials

  • A small bowl or dish (any clean container will do)
  • Salt (sea salt preferred, but table salt works fine)
  • Water (preferably filtered or spring water)
  • A white candle (tea light or small votive)
  • A protective herb (rosemary, bay leaf, or basil â€“ fresh or dried)
  • Optional: a small item to represent yourself (e.g., a coin, stone, or charm)

Steps

  1. Place the bowl or dish in front of you on a clean, stable surface.
  2. Add a small pinch of salt to the water, stirring gently while focusing on your intention of protection.
  3. Add the herb to the mixture, visualizing it strengthening the energy of the spell.
  4. Light the white candle and place it beside the bowl. Take a moment to breathe deeply and focus your thoughts.
  5. If using a personal item, place it near or in the bowl. Speak a protective affirmation or silent prayer, then let the candle burn down safely.
